**Question:** According to Freud in this excerpt, which of the following statements about dreams is considered an illusion?

A) Dreams can solve problems that remain unresolved in waking life.  
B) Dreams are completely uninfluenced by the passage of time.  
C) Dreams have the ability to reflect moral dispositions from waking life.  
D) Dreams can contain more perception content in a short time than waking thoughts can manage.  

**Answer: B**
