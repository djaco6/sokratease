**Question:** According to the text, what is the general consensus about the nature of dreams as discussed by various philosophers and theorists?

A) Dreams operate under strict logical rules similar to waking life.  
B) Dreams can exhibit absurdity and lack of coherent structure, often resembling chaotic thoughts.  
C) Dreams are entirely aimless and meaningless experiences.  
D) Dreams reflect the true desires and rational thoughts of an individual.  

**Answer: B**
