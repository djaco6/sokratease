**Question:** According to the text, what is one of the main differences between dream life and waking life emphasized by G. Th. Fechner?

A) Dreams are simply a continuation of waking ideation at a lower intensity.  
B) The dream scene is located outside the waking presentation life.  
C) Dreams primarily involve voluntary activities rather than involuntary ones.  
D) The psychological peculiarities of dream life cannot be analyzed.  

**Answer:** B
