**Question:** According to the theories discussed in the text, what is the prevailing view of the dream's relationship to psychic activity during sleep?

A) The dream represents a complete cessation of all mental activity.  
B) The dream is the result of a decreased psychic activity that leads to a fragment of consciousness becoming active.  
C) The dream solely reflects external stimuli with no internal psychological processes involved.  
D) The dream is constructed purely from imaginative thoughts with no reliance on prior experiences.  

**Answer: B**
