**Question:** According to the text, what is one of the potential functions of dreams as described by Purkinje?

A) Dreams enhance the logical reasoning skills necessary for waking life.

B) Dreams serve as a form of entertainment and creativity without relating to daily events.

C) Dreams help individuals confront and solve their waking life problems directly.

D) Dreams are a byproduct of brain activity that does not contribute to mental well-being.

**Answer: B**
