**Question:** According to F. W. Hildebrandt's description of dreams, what is the primary relationship between dream content and waking reality?

A) Dreams are completely disconnected and independent from waking reality.  
B) Dream content often has no basis in previous experiences or reality.  
C) Dreams are isolated from reality but still draw upon experiences and thoughts from waking life.  
D) Dreams have no meaningful connection to the dreamer's emotions or past experiences.

**Answer: C**
