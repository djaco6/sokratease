**Question:** According to the text, what is the primary role of dreams in relation to the desires and impulses that we may not acknowledge during our waking state?

A) Dreams serve to entertain and distract us from reality.  
B) Dreams reveal hidden desires and suppressed impulses that are usually inhibited during waking hours.  
C) Dreams are purely random occurrences with no psychological significance.  
D) Dreams confirm our moral and ethical standing in life.

**Answer:** B
