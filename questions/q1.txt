**Question: According to Freud in the excerpt from "Interpretation of Dreams," what does he aim to demonstrate about dreams?**

A) That dreams are purely random and lack any psychological significance.  
B) That dreams can be understood through a psychological technique that assigns meaning to them.  
C) That dreams are primarily a product of divine inspiration and should not be analyzed scientifically.  
D) That the interpretation of dreams has been thoroughly accomplished by earlier authors.  

**Answer: B**
