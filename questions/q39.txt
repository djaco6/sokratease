Which statement best summarizes the relationship between a person's moral character and their dreams, as discussed in the text?

A) Dreams are entirely unrelated to one's moral character and can be influenced by external factors.  
B) A virtuous person will typically dream of virtuous actions, while a sinful person tends to dream of their vices.  
C) Dreams are only reflections of daily events and do not indicate a person's true nature.  
D) The content of dreams is primarily determined by the experiences one encounters while awake.

Answer: B
