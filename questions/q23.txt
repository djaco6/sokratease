**Question: What is a key idea presented by Burdach regarding the nature of dreams in relation to waking life?**

A) Dreams primarily reflect and continue the experiences and thoughts from waking life.  
B) Dreams serve to relieve the dreamer of the trials and joys faced in waking life.  
C) The content of dreams is determined solely by external events and situations.  
D) Dreams are completely isolated from the emotional state of the dreamer during waking hours.  

**Answer: B**
