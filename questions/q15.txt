**Question: What does Freud suggest about the relationship between internal physical states and dreams?**

A) Dreams are solely influenced by external stimuli and do not reflect any internal conditions.  
B) Dreams can often indicate internal organ conditions that the individual may be unaware of in their waking state.  
C) The mind is less aware of the body during sleep than when awake, leading to unrecognized ailments.  
D) Internal organic disturbances have no impact on the emotional content of dreams.

**Answer: B**
